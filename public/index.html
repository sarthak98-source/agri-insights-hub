The Problem We Solve

Agricultural retailers face critical inventory challenges that impact their business and farmers

Stock-Out Crisis

When retailers run out of essential inputs like seeds and fertilizers during peak season, farmers miss critical planting windows, leading to reduced yields.

Overstock Waste

Excess inventory ties up capital and leads to expired products, especially pesticides and fertilizers with limited shelf life.
